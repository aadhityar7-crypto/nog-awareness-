Who We Are

A Grassroots Force
for Good

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit dedicated to improving lives through education, social welfare, environmental conservation, animal welfare, and community development.

Founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, we have grown from a small volunteer circle into a structured organisation running six impactful programmes across India.

Section 8 Registered 80G & 12A Certified CSR-1 Approved ISO 9001:2015 NITI Aayog Listed NGO Darpan
👤
Mr. Govind Shukla
Founder & CEO, InAmigos Foundation
What We Do

Ongoing Projects

Environment

Project Prakriti

Large-scale tree plantation drives and environmental sustainability campaigns for a greener tomorrow.

Social Welfare

Project Seva

Regular food drives and essential support systems for underprivileged communities across the region.

Empowerment

Project Udaan

Awareness campaigns and skill-building workshops to empower girls and women towards independence.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Daily feeding, medical aid, and care for stray and injured animals in urban neighbourhoods.

Education

Project Bachpanshala

Quality education and a nurturing learning environment for underprivileged children across communities.

Skill Development

Project Vikas

Enhancing youth employability through digital literacy and professional training. Over 30,000 interns trained.
